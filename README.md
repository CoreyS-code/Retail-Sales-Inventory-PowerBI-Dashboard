# Retail-Sales-Inventory-PowerBI-Dashboard
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing retail sales, inventory management, and product performance.

## Dataset

**Source:** Kaggle – Retail Sales & Inventory Dataset

The dataset contains retail sales transactions, inventory metrics, product information, pricing, demand forecasts, and category-level performance data used to analyze overall business performance, inventory management, and product performance.

## Objectives

- Analyze overall retail sales performance.
- Monitor inventory trends and stock levels.
- Compare inventory levels with demand forecasts.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Explore relationships between inventory levels and sales.

## Key Findings

- Retail revenue exceeded **$550M** during the reporting period.
- Furniture generated the highest overall revenue among product categories.
- Inventory levels remained relatively consistent throughout the year.
- Products with higher inventory levels generally sold more units, indicating a positive relationship between inventory availability and sales.
